About the BCIS Program

The Bachelor of Computer Information Systems (BCIS) at Mount Royal University integrates the disciplines of business, computing, and information processing. Our commitment to hands-on learning and real-world experiences prepares our graduates to be ahead in the field from day one.

Program Highlights

Our BCIS program offers a unique blend of practical technology skills with a business focus, allowing our students to understand and adapt to the evolving needs of the industry. Key areas of study include:

  • Software Engineering
  • Systems Analysis
  • Web and Mobile Application Development
  • Data Management and Analytics
  • IT Project Management

With a variety of elective options, students can tailor their education to their interests and career aspirations.

Our Faculty

Our faculty members are not only educators but also active professionals and researchers in the field of information systems. This ensures that the knowledge and expertise they bring to the classroom are rooted in industry practice and current trends.
